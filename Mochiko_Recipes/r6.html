Matar Paneer

What is Matar Paneer?

Matar Paneer is a popular vegetarian North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and green peas in a tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Gopi or Haldiram’s, soak in hot water for 20 minutes)

  • Frozen green peas (run under warm water)

  • Fresh tomatoes

  • Red onions

  • Cilantro leaves (coriander leaves)

  • Ginger garlic paste or grated ginger and garlic

  • Cumin seeds, Cloves, Salt

  • Green cardamom pods

  • Turmeric powder

  • Kashmiri red chili powder (paprika)

  • Red chili powder (cayenne)

  • Coriander powder

  • Garam masala powder

  • Kasoori methi (dry fenugreek leaves, rub before adding)

Recipe

Step 1:

Heat the Pan

Heat up a nonstick pan on medium heat, and add 1 tablespoon oil.

Step 2:

Add the Paneer

Once hot, add cubed paneer.

Step 3:

Sauté the Paneer

Sauté the paneer for 2-3 minutes max until it’s light golden brown. DO NOT OVERCOOK THE PANEER, or else it’ll become rubbery.

Step 4:

Drain Excess Oil

Once done, transfer the paneer onto a plate with a paper towel. This will remove some of that excess oil.

Step 5:

Sauté the Spices

Heat a pan on medium heat. Once hot, add oil. Then add cumin seeds, green cardamom, and cloves. Sauté for about 30 seconds.

Step 6:

Add Green Chilies

Next, add the sliced green chilies. Sauté for 30 seconds or so.

Step 7:

Cook the Onions

Add the red onions and season with salt. Stir. Allow the onions to cook for about 3-5 minutes or until golden, stirring occasionally. You may need to add a 1/4 cup of water to deglaze the pan.

Step 8:

Add Ginger and Garlic

Now add the grated ginger and garlic.

Step 9:

Sauté Ginger and Garlic

Stir and sauté for a minute.

Step 10:

Add Pureed Tomatoes

Add the pureed tomatoes. Stir for a minute.

Step 11:

Add Ground Spices

Add the ground spices – turmeric powder, Kashmiri chili powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Stir well.

Step 12:

Cook the Mixture

Cover and cook for 15 minutes on low-medium heat until you have a paste-like consistency.

Step 13:

Achieve Paste-Like Consistency

This is what you are looking for – a paste-like consistency. This step is important so make sure the result is like a tomato paste with no more liquid.

Step 14:

Add Water

Add a cup of water. Stir well.

Step 15:

Add Green Peas

Add the green peas. Stir.

Step 16:

Cook the Peas

Cover and cook for 4-5 minutes on low-medium heat.

Step 17:

Flavor with Kasoori Methi and Garam Masala

Stir the sauce. Rub kasoori methi between the palms of your hands for maximum flavor and add it to your sauce. Also, add garam masala.

Step 18:

Add Sauteed Paneer

Add the sautéed paneer.

Step 19:

Garnish with Fresh Cilantro

Garnish with lots of fresh cilantro.

Step 20:

Serve and Enjoy

Enjoy your Matar Paneer with roti, naan, or rice.

Variations

  • Creamy Matar Paneer: Add a splash of heavy cream or coconut cream to the sauce for a richer texture.

  • Paneer Tikka Masala Style:Marinate the paneer cubes in yogurt and tikka masala spices before sautéing them. Then proceed with the recipe as usual.

  • Spicy Matar Paneer:Increase the amount of green chilies or add a pinch of cayenne pepper for extra heat.

  • Healthy Matar Paneer:Use tofu instead of paneer for a vegan version, or reduce the amount of oil used for sautéing.
