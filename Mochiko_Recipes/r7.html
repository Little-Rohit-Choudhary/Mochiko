Litchi Juice

What is Litchi Juice?

Litchi juice is a beverage made from the fruit of the litchi tree (Litchi chinensis), which is native to Southeast Asia.

Ingredients

  • Fresh litchies

  • Ice cubes (optional)

  • 8 Oreo cookies (or other chocolate sandwich cookies)

  • Mint leaves or lemon slices for garnish (optional)

Recipe

Step 1:

Prepare the Litchies

Separate the litchies from the bunch and wash them thoroughly. Pat dry with a kitchen towel.

Step 2:

Peel the Litchies

Gently peel the skin, keeping the flesh intact.

Step 3:

Remove Seeds and Slice

Remove the seeds and tear the litchi flesh into slices.

Step 4:

Blend to Puree

Put the litchi flesh in a mixer jar and blend it to a puree to make a smoothie.

Step 5:

Chill the Puree

Transfer the puree to a wide vessel and refrigerate it for about an hour.

Step 6:

Serve Chilled and Garnish

After chilling, pour the litchi juice into glasses and serve chilled. Optionally, add an ice cube on top. While serving, garnish with a few mint leaves or lemon slices for added freshness.

Variations

  • Flavor Variations: You can experiment with different ice cream flavors, such as chocolate or cookies and cream, for a different twist on the classic Oreo milkshake.

  • Thickness: Adjust the amount of milk to control the thickness of your milkshake. Less milk will make it thicker, while more milk will make it thinner.

  • Extras: Add a splash of vanilla extract or a tablespoon of chocolate syrup for extra flavor.
