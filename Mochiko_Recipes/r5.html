Oreo Milkshake

What is Oreo Milkshake?

An Oreo milkshake is a delicious and indulgent beverage made by blending Oreo cookies with milk and ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 pint vanilla ice cream

  • 1 cup milk (preferably whole milk)

  • 8 Oreo cookies (or other chocolate sandwich cookies)

  • 1 tablespoon chocolate sauce

  • 2 Oreo cookies, garnish

Recipe

Step 1:

Gather the ingredients

Crush the Oreo cookies slightly for easier blending.

Step 2:

Blend

Place the vanilla ice cream, milk, 8 Oreo cookies, and the chocolate sauce in a blender and puree until smooth.

Step 3:

Garnish

Crush the remaining 2 Oreo cookies for the garnish by placing them in a zip-close plastic bag and pounding on them a few times with a rolling pin until they crumble.

Step 4:

Serve

Pour the milkshakes into 2 tall glasses and top each with the crushed Oreo cookie crumbs. Garnish with a straw.

Variations

  • Flavor Variations: You can experiment with different ice cream flavors, such as chocolate or cookies and cream, for a different twist on the classic Oreo milkshake.

  • Thickness: Adjust the amount of milk to control the thickness of your milkshake. Less milk will make it thicker, while more milk will make it thinner.

  • Extras: Add a splash of vanilla extract or a tablespoon of chocolate syrup for extra flavor.
