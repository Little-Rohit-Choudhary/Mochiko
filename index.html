About us

Welcome to Mochiko

At Mochiko, we believe that cooking is an art form and that everyone can be an artist in the kitchen. Our website was created with one mission in mind: to inspire and empower home cooks of all skill levels to create delicious, beautiful dishes.

Our Story

The idea for Mochiko began in 2024 when our founder, Ryoichi Tanaka, realized that while there were countless recipe websites, few offered a blend of authenticity, creativity, and practicality. Drawing from a rich culinary heritage and a passion for food, Ryoichi Tanaka decided to create a platform that celebrates both traditional and innovative recipes.

Our Mission

Our mission at Mochiko is to make cooking accessible, enjoyable, and rewarding for everyone. We are committed to:

  • Quality Recipes :

    Each recipe on our site is meticulously tested to ensure it works perfectly every time.

  • Diversity :

    We celebrate global cuisines and offer a wide variety of recipes, from everyday meals to gourmet creations.

  • Education :

    Through detailed instructions, step-by-step photos, and video tutorials, we aim to educate and inspire our users to expand their culinary horizons.
